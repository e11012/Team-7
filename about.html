ABOUT US

Philology is a great resource for high schoolers to connect with others across the world. We aim to bring Foreign Exchanges between students to an online platform. With sharing of ideas, it will be a worthwhile experience for the children.

Engage and learn about each other using our survey which will match you and a student from the respective school.Using our platform,it is extremely accessible and easy to collaborate on projects and work in groups.
